GodSlayers

Godslayers was the game I worked on as part of my capstone project at the University of California, Santa Cruz. Our team worked on developing this over the course of 5 months where I served as the main programmer. The project was made in Godot, and I created and implemented all systems with the exception of character tweens and the dialogue addon used for dialogue segments.
