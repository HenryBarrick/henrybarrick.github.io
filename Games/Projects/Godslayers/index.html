GodSlayers

Godslayers was the game I worked on as part of my capstone project at the University of California, Santa Cruz. Our team worked on developing this over the course of 5 months where I served as the main programmer. The project was made using Godot. Over the course of the project I was responsible for creating the majority of code that would be used in our project, implementation of assets our artists created, and managing our version control on Githb.
