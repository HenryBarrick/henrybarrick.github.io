Moth Game

Moth game was the first fully completed solo project I have made. It had a very short development cycle, beginning development in early October and finishing development in mid December. As my first attempt at a finished game, the scale was intended to be relatively small, mainly being a walking simulator across a large cliffside. This decision was made out of an attempt

Being tasked to do this in a way I was mostly unfamiliar with, coupled with my senior year at University and being unfamiliar both with making finished games and the Godot engine ment that this came out with some very rough edges. Ultimately this is a project I am not the most proud of but serves as a good basis for improvement in the future.

This game has no publicly avaliable download at the moment. This is because the game was audio balanced to be played in a public showcase like setting and one of the sound effects is not balanced correctly which may pose a risk to a players ears. In a show of stupidity I did not keep a backup of the finished project before removing it from my hard drive, a mistake I have since learned to not repeat. I still retain a copy of the finished executable, if you want a copy please email me.
