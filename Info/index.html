About Me

Henry Tripp Barrick is a 3D artist and game developer currently studying at the University of California, Santa Cruz currently majoring in Anthropology and Arts and Design: Games and Playable Media. Henry works primarily in creating hard surface 3D models and retro-style assets, and has worked in both Godot and Unreal Engine 5. Henry initially began making 3D models while creating mods for video games, which eventually spiraled into developing his own games. He has developed "Ship Game" and worked in a team to develop "Godslayers" and "Patient 004" serving as both a programmer and a 3D artist in a retro style. Henry aims to develop games which are enjoyable, unique, and weird; and which create inspirational systems and environments.
